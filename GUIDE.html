An experiment in machine taste

The Ghost
Client Test

Can an AI hear a brand in the way a person talks, before anyone says a word about design?

Aexagon · July 2026
The idea

Most branding starts with a moodboard and a guess. We wanted the opposite: every design choice traced to something the client actually said.

So we built a pipeline that listens first. Then we tested whether it truly listens, with a client who cannot be googled, because he does not exist.

The method
01

Generate the ghost

One agent invents a person. Dee, 29, Singapore hawker-food creator. It writes his raw interview transcripts, messy and real, and a sealed answer key stating the feelings his brand should give off.

Rule: the transcripts never mention design. Dee talks about steam and aunties, not hex codes.
02

Derive, blind

A second agent reads only the transcripts. No answer key, no design library, no context. From voice alone it derives palette temperature, concrete colours, type, layout grammar, and what the brand must never be.

Rule: every pick cites a verbatim quote. No vibes-only choices.
03

Judge

The derivation is scored against the sealed key it never saw. Tone words, temperature, type energy, banned moves, and the one feeling a visitor should get.

Rule: partial credit only for spirit. Verbatim recovery is the bar.
04

Build

The derived direction becomes a full brand identity document that demonstrates the system it describes. Hot orange field, poster type, packed layout. A brand book that shouts, because he does.

Rule: the doc carries its reasons. Each choice states its why, in his words.
Why the test is honest

The key is sealed

The intended answers exist before derivation starts, written down where the deriving agent cannot see them. No moving the goalposts after.

The deriver is blind

It gets two transcript files. Nothing else. It cannot pattern-match a house style it has never seen.

The result
Three tone words, recovered verbatim: fun, loud, honestHit
Palette temperature: bright, saturated, warm. Flame orange, chilli red, turmeric, one pandan popHit
Type energy: poster-shout display, friendly human body faceHit
All three forbidden directions caught from his own rantsHit
The feeling: a loud 7am wet market, a friend pulling you in. Same image, reached independentlyHit

Five for five. And on a real client, run the same way, the pipeline rebuilt a shipped design system from raw audio transcripts alone.

Run it yourself

You need any capable AI agent and an hour.

  1. Have it invent a person and write their interview transcripts, plus a sealed answer key it saves for later. Feelings only, no design words.
  2. In a fresh context, hand over just the transcripts. Ask for colours, type, and layout, each choice traced to a quote.
  3. Compare against the key. Grade hard.
  4. If it passes, make it build the brand book. If it fails, you learned your agent decorates instead of listens.
